My Father Does he understand me...

"Dear Diary,

Today, I want to share a deeply personal experience, one that revolves around a significant trauma I faced. It is a story that reflects the lack of understanding from my father, which added to the weight of my burden.

Growing up, I longed for my father's support and understanding. However, the void between us seemed insurmountable. For some reason, he couldn't grasp the pain and struggles I carried within me.

One particular traumatic incident left an indelible mark on my life. The memory of it often haunts me, leaving me feeling isolated and unheard. I was hoping that sharing my pain with my father would bring solace, but instead, it only intensified my feelings of being lost in a sea of darkness.

There were nights when I would cry myself to sleep, craving my father's empathetic presence, yearning for him to understand the depth of my sorrow. But time and time again, my attempts to bridge the gap between us fell on deaf ears, making me feel as though my pain was invisible, almost inconsequential.

This lack of understanding took a toll on my emotional well-being, deepening the wounds that were already struggling to heal. It seemed as though I were carrying this heavy burden alone, desperately seeking validation and understanding, only to be met with silence and indifference.

However, through this journey, I have discovered my own resilience and strength. I've learned to become my own source of support, finding solace within myself and seeking solace from trusted friends and loved ones who understand the complexities of my experiences.

My heart still longs for the day when my father will truly understand and acknowledge the pain I carry. But until then, I will continue to lean on the support system I have built for myself, finding solace in those who genuinely empathize with my struggles.

Dear Diary, sharing this trauma with you brings a sense of release and catharsis. It is a reminder that our stories matter, even if those closest to us may not fully understand. May my journey inspire others to seek healing and find solace within themselves, even in the absence of understanding from those they hold dear.

With strength and resilience,
Adi,
